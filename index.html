About Ethiopia

Ethiopia is one of the world's oldest nations, rich in culture, history, and natural beauty.

History of Ethiopia

From the Aksumite Empire to the victory at Adwa and the modern federal system, Ethiopia's history spans thousands of years.

Geography

Ethiopia features the Great Rift Valley, high mountains, lowlands, and one of the most diverse landscapes in Africa.

Economy

The economy is based on agriculture, industry, and services, with the GERD project being a major national investment.

Regional States

Ethiopia has 11 regions and 2 chartered cities, including Addis Ababa and Dire Dawa.

Religion

Major religions include Ethiopian Orthodox Christianity, Islam, Protestant Christianity, and traditional beliefs.
